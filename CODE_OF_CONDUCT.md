# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in Naxora welcoming and harassment-free for everyone, regardless of background, identity, appearance, experience, or ability.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, constructive, and patient.
- Give feedback about the work, not the person.
- Respect privacy and never share another person's images or personal information.
- Avoid language or behavior that is discriminatory, sexualized, threatening, or demeaning.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit or remove contributions and comments that violate these standards and may temporarily or permanently ban contributors for inappropriate behavior. Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainers through the repository's available private contact channel.
